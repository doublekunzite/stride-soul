FAQ

Answers to our most frequently asked questions.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location. You can see specific estimates at checkout.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number on the carrier's website to follow your package's journey.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, in the original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase. Please visit our Shipping & Returns page for more details.

Are your shoes true to size?

Most of our models fit true to size. However, we recommend checking the specific product page or our Size Guide for recommendations on specific models like the Trail Blazer, which runs slightly small.
