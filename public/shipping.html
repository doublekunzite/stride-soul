Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about delivery and exchanges.

Shipping Policy

We offer standard and expedited shipping options.

Domestic (US):
Standard Shipping (5-7 Business Days): Free on orders over $100.
Expedited (2-3 Business Days): $15.00.

International:
Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. Please allow 10-14 business days for international delivery.

Return Policy

We want you to love your new shoes. If you are not completely satisfied, you may return your order within 30 days of purchase.

Conditions for Return:

  • Item must be unworn and in original condition.
  • Original packaging (shoe box) must be intact.
  • Proof of purchase required.

To initiate a return, please visit our Contact page with your order number.
