Running Green

Our commitment to the planet.

Eco-Friendly Materials

We are transitioning 80% of our product line to use recycled polyester and bio-based foams. Our laces are made from post-consumer plastic, and our insoles utilize algae bloom foam.

Carbon Neutral Shipping

Every order placed on Stride & Soul is shipped carbon-neutral. We partner with logistics providers to offset emissions and use packaging that is 100% recyclable and plastic-free.

The Goal

By 2027, we aim to have a fully circular product lifecycle, allowing customers to return worn shoes for recycling or refurbishment.
